2D Heat Transfer Model with Smoothed Step Function

Interactive simulation of heat transfer in a rectangular domain with time-dependent boundary conditions.

Parameters in this model:
fmin: Initial temperature (200-500 K)
fmax: Final temperature (300-2000 K)
ts: Transition time (0-600 s)
m: Smoothness factor (0.001-0.1)
k: Thermal conductivity (0.1-10 W/m·K)
Tamb: Ambient temperature (200-500 K)
h: Convection coefficient (0-200 W/m²·K)
t: Current time slider
